The problem

We don't have a willpower problem. We have a design problem.

Feeds are engineered by thousand-person teams to be unputdownable. Your built-in screen-time limit is one tap to dismiss — so it gets dismissed. The result is a quiet, daily kind of guilt.

🌀

The scroll is compulsive

The average phone gets unlocked 100+ times a day. It isn't a choice — it's a reflex wired straight into your dopamine system.

🚫

Blockers create a void

Hard blockers just slam a door. With nothing to fill the gap, you tap "ignore limit" and relapse in seconds. Willpower-only doesn't scale.

🌍

You want to keep up

You mean to stay informed — but the feed wins every time. The intention to know what's going on quietly loses to the algorithm.

Why we built it

What if the thing standing between you and your feed wasn't a wall — but a trade?

Sharp doesn't punish the urge. It redirects it. The same impulse that opens Instagram now buys you 90 seconds of genuinely knowing what's happening in the world. You don't lose time — you trade it up. The block becomes a reward.

  • ✓ Replaces the void with a fast, satisfying micro-win
  • ✓ Builds a real habit through streaks, not shame
  • ✓ Fresh current-affairs content every day, tuned to you
